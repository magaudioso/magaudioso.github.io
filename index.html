MNIST Dataset Classifier

The MNIST dataset is known as the "Hello World" of Machine Learning and forward Neural Networks. I wanted to create a classifier that would accurately predict the correct integer when inputted a 28x28 "handwrititen" image of a written. I wanted to beat the average validation accuracy of 92%, as stated in the TensorFlow documentation. I did that, and obtained 97.74% accuracy for my model.

Audiobook Classifier

I found a customer research sample as part of a Data Science Udemy Course I completed. The file is saved under my github as Audiobook.csv. My goal was to classify a customer as "retained" or "not retained" based on a variety of inputs such as money spent, length of book, and other customer features. I was able to build a model to reach 80% accuracy, after sucessfully preprocessing the data.
